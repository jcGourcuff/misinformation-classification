Jordan Lee - Clueless about Climate Change

Background: Jordan is a 28-year-old graphic designer who has heard about climate change in the news but has never delved into the details. They are curious and open to learning but have not sought out information on the topic.

Knowledge Level: Beginner. Jordan has a basic awareness that climate change is an issue but lacks understanding of the causes, effects, and potential solutions. They might confuse weather with climate and are unaware of the scientific consensus on the subject.

Attitude: Open-minded and willing to learn. Jordan is not skeptical about climate change but simply hasn't had the opportunity or motivation to educate themselves on the topic. They are interested in simple explanations and practical steps they can take to make a difference.
